Privacy Policy for SOUL

Last updated: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SOUL ("the App", "the Game"), developed by Webbnov, handles information. We respect your privacy and aim to collect as little information as possible.

1. Information We Collect

SOUL does not require you to create an account, and does not collect any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location).

The App may collect limited, anonymous, non-identifying usage data to help us understand how the game is played and to improve it. This may include:

  • Anonymous gameplay events (e.g. game started, game over, world reached, achievement unlocked, skin equipped)
  • Session information (e.g. session length, score, world progression)
  • General technical information (e.g. device type, operating system version, country/region — derived automatically from network information, not GPS)

This data is not linked to your identity and cannot be used to personally identify you. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, photos, or any other personal files.

2. How We Use This Information

Anonymous usage data, if collected, is used solely to:

  • Understand how players progress through the game's worlds
  • Identify technical issues or points where players stop playing
  • Improve game balance, content, and overall player experience

We do not sell, rent, or share this data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

SOUL may use Supabase, a backend service provider, to store anonymous gameplay statistics described above. Supabase acts strictly as a data processor and does not have independent rights to use this data. You can learn more about Supabase's own data practices at supabase.com/privacy.

SOUL does not currently display advertising and does not use third-party advertising networks.

4. Data Storage and Security

Anonymous usage data, if collected, is stored securely using industry-standard practices. Local game progress (such as unlocked skins and achievements) is stored only on your device during a session and is not automatically transmitted unless you explicitly use the in-game export/import feature, which produces a text code under your control.

5. Children's Privacy

SOUL does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone, including children. The game is suitable for general audiences and contains no personal data collection that would require parental consent.

6. Your Choices

Since SOUL does not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal account or profile to access, modify, or delete. Anonymous gameplay statistics, where collected, cannot be traced back to you individually.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if new features (such as optional contact lists, in-app purchases, or advertising) are introduced. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date, and corresponding disclosures will be updated in the app's store listing where required.
